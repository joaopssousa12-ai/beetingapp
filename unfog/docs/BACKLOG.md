# Unfog — Feature Backlog (council-vetted)

Every item was pressure-tested against the 5-advisor council and the one test that
matters for this audience (First-Principles): **does it lower the cost to START, or
the cost to DECIDE, for a brain with an executive-function deficit?** If not, it's cut.

Legend — effort: S (hours) · M (a day) · L (multi-day). Champion = the advisor who pushed for it.

## Shipped ✅
- **Brain dump → AI microsteps** (first step < 2 min). *First-Principles.*
- **One-thing-at-a-time "Now"** view. *First-Principles.*
- **Plan my Day** — energy-aware AI time-blocking. *Expansionist / user.*
- **Spiciness slider** (chill / normal / tiny) — Goblin Tools' best idea. *Outsider.*
- **Close the day** — shame-free evening reflection. *Contrarian (retention).*
- **Wins** — streaks that bend, don't break. *Contrarian.*
- **Founding-member** willingness-to-pay test. *Outsider.*

## P0 — next (retention + validation; the council says this beats new features)
1. **Daily reminder notifications** (web push) — the #1 churn fix. Bring people back to
   the "plan my day" ritual. *Executor.* Effort M. **(approved, building)**
2. **A companion that grows with you** (the Finch mechanic) — a small plant/creature that
   levels up as you do steps; never guilts you on off-days. This is BOTH the retention
   unlock AND the free marketing engine (people screenshot & share it). *Expansionist.* Effort L.
3. **Shareable cards** — auto-generate a pretty image of "my plan today" / "my week of wins"
   the user can post. Product-as-marketing, exactly how Finch went viral. *Outsider.* Effort M.

## P1 — strong, cheap wins stolen from competitors
4. **Voice capture** for the brain dump (speak instead of type) — every retention study says
   ADHD capture must be frictionless/voice. *First-Principles.* Effort M.
5. **"Don't overcommit" guard** on Plan — warn when the day is over-stuffed vs waking hours
   (Sunsama's capacity warning). *Contrarian.* Effort S.
6. **Time estimator + "reality check"** — after a task, ask how long it really took; learn the
   user's personal fudge factor for better future plans (Sunsama time-tracking + ADHD time
   blindness). *First-Principles.* Effort M.
7. **Tone helper / "soften this message"** (Goblin Tools' Formalizer) — paste an awkward text,
   get a kind version. Beloved, viral, tiny. *Outsider.* Effort S.
8. **Recurring routines / anchors** (morning, wind-down) — Tiimo's core. *Executor.* Effort M.

## P2 — bigger bets, only after product-market-fit
9. **Focus rooms / body doubling** — Focusmate's 85%-completion magic. Needs a two-sided
   pool; start as a scheduled "co-working timer" with a live count of others focusing. *Expansionist.* Effort L.
10. **Google Calendar sync** — read real events so the plan flows around meetings. *Expansionist.* Effort L.
11. **Body/energy check-in** — "how's your battery?" reshapes the day. The 2026 differentiator
    almost nobody nails. *First-Principles.* Effort M.
12. **Coach / therapist B2B2C** — practitioners assign Unfog to clients. Real revenue, later. *Expansionist.* Effort L.

## Explicitly NOT building (the Contrarian's kills)
- Heavy manual project management (boards, tags, dependencies) — demands the exact executive
  function our users lack. That's the graveyard.
- Anything with red "overdue" states or guilt mechanics.
- A social feed — moderation cost + distraction, no clear retention lift for us.
